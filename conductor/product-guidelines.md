# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style and Tone
- **Professional and Concise:** Use direct, clear, and business-like language throughout the application.

## Visual Identity
- **Information Density:** Use a compact and efficient layout to maximize information density.
- **Color Scheme:** Strictly follow the default Material 3 color scheme.
- **Feedback & Delight:** Prioritize visual delight and feedback, focusing on smooth animations and transitions for UI interactions.

## Empty States & Errors
- **Minimalist:** Use brief notifications or icons for error messages and empty states.
